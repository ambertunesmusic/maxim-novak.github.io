Maxim Novak, composer

Maxim Novak is a composer of instrumental music with a nostalgic, cinematic touch — blending elements of jazz, bossa nova, retro, and folk traditions. His melodies have been featured in multiple documentary films, hundreds of YouTube and TikTok videos, and artistic collaborations with creators around the world. His music is available on platforms like Free Music Archive and Spotify. In 2024, the Free Music Archive published a dedicated article highlighting his unique emotional and melodic style. He is also listed on IMDb for his contributions to film scoring.
